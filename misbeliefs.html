Misbeliefs

Good characters are the most important part of a story, and good misbeliefs are the most important piece to a good character. Since I have failed to find such a resource anywhere else, enjoy my misbelief thesaurus (always a work in progress).

  • The only person worth looking out for is yourself.

  • All people are awful at their core, everyone will betray me.

  • People are pawns to be used and manipulated.

  • Life is about survival, and I am doomed to violence.

  • I am dangerous/a monster, I can never be safe/normal.

  • My worth is in my service/I am a tool (usually because of a power), not as an individual.

  • I'm not worthy of love and respect, unless I prove myself.

  • When you let people close, they only hurt you. / No one truly loves me, and will only hurt me if I let them in.
